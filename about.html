Raised wild, rooted in family

ROWEM is a family-owned shop inspired by life outside. Our name comes from our kids, Rowdy and Remy, and everything we build starts at home with them.

Who We Are

A family that thrives outside

We started ROWEM because the outdoor life is not something we talk about. It is how we spend our days. Slow mornings, backyard adventures, time in the field. We wanted a place to share the goods we reach for ourselves: pieces that feel at home in nature and comfortable at the end of a long one.

Our name comes from our children's names, Rowdy and Remy. They are the heart of what we do, and every collection we build starts with what we would want for our own home.

What We Carry

Goods we know and stand behind

As a small family business, we share products from makers we know and use, alongside pieces we are designing ourselves. You will find bamboo children's clothing, field gear, outdoor play toys, and farm and ranch books for young readers.

We only carry what we believe in. If our family loves it, it earns a place here.

Coming From ROWEM

Apparel made by our family

Creating our own children's clothing has been a long-held dream. We are launching bamboo zip footies, two-piece sets, and matching bows with custom prints you will not find anywhere else.

Outdoor kids' clothing should hold up to muddy backyards and still feel soft at bedtime. That is the standard we build to.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about orders, sizing, and our products. Still need help? Get in touch.

What sizes do you carry for children's apparel?

ROWEM bamboo apparel is available in newborn through 5T, including zip footies and two-piece sets. Visit any apparel product page for sizing details, or email us if you need help choosing a fit.

When will my pre-order ship?

Pre-orders are produced in small batches from our family studio in Papillion, NE. You will receive an estimated ship window after checkout and another email when your order is on its way.

What makes bamboo clothing a good choice for kids?

Bamboo is naturally soft, breathable, and gentle on sensitive skin. Great for little ones who go straight from nap time to the backyard.

Do you ship outside the United States?

We currently ship within the United States. For special requests, reach out to sales@rowemproducts.com and we will do our best to help.

What is your return and exchange policy?

Unworn items in original condition may be returned within 30 days of delivery. Contact our team to start a return or exchange. We want you to love what you ordered.

How should I care for ROWEM bamboo apparel?

Machine wash cold on gentle and tumble dry low or line dry. Avoid bleach and high heat to keep fibers soft and colors true wash after wash.

Who are the ROWEM books written for?

Our eleven titles by Stephanie White are written for young readers curious about farm life, hunting, the outdoors, and journaling. Most are ideal for ages 4–10; see each book's product page for more detail.

How do I contact customer service?

Email sales@rowemproducts.com or call 402-322-0491. We are a small family team and read every message.
