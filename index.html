Life Outside

Rowem is a small family business inspired by life outside. We create everyday bamboo apparel for kids, carry field gear we actually use, and stock farm, ranch, and hunting books young readers love.

Rowem started at our kitchen table with Rowdy and Remy, the two kids behind our name. We began with bamboo zippy's we wished existed for them: tough enough for muddy backyards, soft enough for bedtime. Read our story.
