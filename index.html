Our Mission

ROWEM is a small family shop rooted in life outside. We make bamboo clothes for kids, carry field gear we actually use, and stock farm and ranch books young readers love.

ROWEM started at our kitchen table with Rowdy and Remy, the two kids behind our name. We began with bamboo pieces we wished existed for them: tough enough for muddy backyards, soft enough for bedtime. Read our story.
