Privacy Policy

We are a small family shop in Greenwood, NE. We only collect what we need to run the store and take care of your orders.

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

When you shop with us or reach out, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address
  • Order details, including items purchased and payment confirmation (we do not store full credit card numbers)
  • Messages you send us by email or through our contact form
  • Basic site usage data, like pages visited and browser type, to help us improve the shopping experience

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process and ship orders, answer questions, send order updates, and improve our website and products. We do not sell your personal information. We only share what is needed to run the business, such as with our payment processor and shipping carriers.

Cookies

Our site may use cookies and similar tools to remember your cart, keep you signed in, and understand how people use the site. You can adjust cookie settings in your browser at any time. Some parts of the site may not work as expected if cookies are turned off.

Third Parties

We work with trusted partners to operate the store, including payment processing, shipping, and website hosting. These partners only receive the information they need to do their job and are expected to protect it. We may also use analytics tools to see how visitors use our site in aggregate.

Children's Privacy

ROWEM sells children's products, but our website is meant for adults making purchases. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has shared information with us, please contact us and we will remove it.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your data? We are happy to help.
