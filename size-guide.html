Size Guide

Our bamboo apparel runs newborn through 5T. Sizes are true to standard US children's sizing. When in doubt, size up for room to grow.

Fit Notes

ROWEM sizes run true to standard US children's sizing. Bamboo has a gentle stretch, so pieces should feel comfortable without being tight. Every child grows differently, so if your little one is between sizes or tall for their age, we recommend sizing up.

Still unsure? Contact us with your child's weight and height and we will help you pick the right size.
