Size Guide

Our bamboo apparel runs 0-3M through 5/6. Bamboo Zippy's are sized 0-3M–2T, two-piece sets 2T–5/6, and bows are one size (0-3M–3T). Bamboo clothing should fit snug. Sizes are true to standard US children's sizing.

Fit Notes

ROWEM sizes run true to standard US children's sizing. Bamboo clothing should fit snug — the gentle stretch is meant to hug comfortably, not hang loose.

Still unsure? Contact us with your child's weight and height and we will help you pick the right size.
